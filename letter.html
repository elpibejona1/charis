Dear Delegates,

We have been asked to prepare the schedule for our coming conference. We are excited to see you all in Bangkok! Please read the following carefully to ensure a great conference!

Here is some information / preparations:

One person per continent (We suggest: Paris Peña for South America, Louis Romtomne for Africa, Malcolm Stevens for Europe, Shu Aoki for Asia, Bartley Sawatsky for North America) will introduce the delegates per country in a total of 10 minutes max. It is up to this person to gather the facts☺.

Each delegate will bring something “fun” (one object) and representative of each country: something artistic, clothing, or food (no, the French people may not bring cheese, it smells too bad!).

Each country should prepare TWO POWER POINTS (to be sent Oct 15th to Florent)

  • One PowerPoint of 1 slide (ONLY) showing where are the GBC in that country. It will be presented on Monday.
  • One PowerPoint of 3 to 5 slides that show the progress made since the last Charis conference on church planting, leadership training, and integrated ministry. It will be presented on Wednesday.

Finally, if you can talk in a workshop on a specialty, or a theme that you think would benefit other Charis people, let us know. We will have workshops on Tuesday and Wednesday and the more popular themes will be proposed on both days. Your suggestion must be sent to Florent by October 1st.)

You will see the proposed schedule in the "Conference Schedule" section of this web site. Don’t hesitate to ask questions if something needs to be clarified.

Greetings in the Lord from the three of us,

Eduardo P (Argentina)

Augustin H (C.A.R.)

Florent V (France)
